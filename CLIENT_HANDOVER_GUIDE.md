# Client Handover Guide

Welcome! This guide will walk you through setting up the accounts needed for your website and granting access. Please follow each section carefully.

---

## 📋 Overview

Your website requires three main services:
1. **Vercel** - For hosting and deploying your website
2. **Resend** - For sending emails (newsletter signups, contact forms)
3. **Contentful CMS** - For managing your blog posts and content

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Create Vercel Account

Vercel is where your website will be hosted and automatically deployed.

### Instructions:

1. **Sign Up:**
   - Go to [https://vercel.com](https://vercel.com)
   - Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
   - Choose to sign up with **GitHub**, **GitLab**, or **Bitbucket** (recommended: GitHub)
   - Follow the authentication process

2. **Complete Your Profile:**
   - Add your name and company information (optional)

### Granting Access:

You have two options to grant access:

**Option 1: Share Account Credentials (Free Plan)**
- Share your Vercel account email address and password
- This allows direct access to your account

**Option 2: Add as Team Member (Requires Pro Plan)**
- If you have a Vercel Pro plan, you can add a team member
- Go to your Vercel dashboard → **Settings** → **Team** → **Invite Member**
- Enter the email address provided and send the invitation

**Please confirm which method you'll use.**

---

## 📧 Step 2: Create Resend Account

Resend handles all email sending from your website (newsletter signups, contact forms, etc.).

### Instructions:

1. **Sign Up:**
   - Go to [https://resend.com](https://resend.com)
   - Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
   - Enter your email address and create a password
   - Or sign up with Google/GitHub if available

2. **Complete Account Setup:**
   - Fill in any required profile information
   - Accept the terms of service

3. **Add Team Member:**
   - Once logged in, go to **Settings** → **Teams**
   - Click **"Add Member"** or **"Invite Member"**
   - Enter the email address provided
   - Select an appropriate role
   - Send the invitation
   - An invitation email will be sent to join your Resend team

**Note:** All technical setup including API keys and domain configuration will be handled. You just need to grant access to your account.

---

## 📝 Step 3: Create Contentful CMS Account

Contentful is where you'll manage your blog posts and website content (no coding required!).

### Instructions:

1. **Sign Up:**
   - Go to [https://www.contentful.com](https://www.contentful.com)
   - Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
   - Choose the **Free** plan (perfect for getting started)
   - Enter your email address and create a password
   - Or sign up with Google/GitHub if available

2. **Note About Default Organization and Space:**
   - When you create a Contentful account, a default organization and space are automatically created for you
   - You can use this default space, or create a new one if you prefer
   - The content structure will be set up in the space

3. **Add Team Member to Your Organization:**
   - Go to [https://app.contentful.com/account/profile/organization_memberships](https://app.contentful.com/account/profile/organization_memberships)
   - Click on your organization name to open **Organization Settings**
   - In the navigation bar, click on **Users**
   - Click the **"Invite User"** button
   - Enter the email address provided
   - Select an appropriate role
   - Send the invitation

4. **Add Team Member to Your Space:**
   - Make sure you're inside your Space (the default space or the one you created)
   - Look for the **Settings icon** (gear icon) at the top right, near your user profile
   - Click on the **Settings icon** and in the dropdown menu, select **Users**
   - You can add users who are already members of your organization
   - Click **"Add Member"** or **"Invite Member"**
   - Enter the email address provided (must be someone already in your organization)
   - Select an appropriate role (typically "Editor" or "Admin")
   - Send the invitation

**Note:** All technical setup including API keys, webhooks, and content model configuration will be handled. You just need to grant access to your organization and space.


## ✅ Checklist: Granting Access

Once you've completed all three steps, confirm completion:

### Vercel:
- [ ] Account created
- [ ] Access method confirmed (credentials shared OR team member added if Pro plan)

### Resend:
- [ ] Account created
- [ ] Team member added (Settings → Teams)
- [ ] Invitation accepted

### Contentful:
- [ ] Account created
- [ ] Team member added to organization (via https://app.contentful.com/account/profile/organization_memberships → Organization Settings → Users)
- [ ] Team member added to space (Space → Settings → Members)
- [ ] Both invitations accepted
